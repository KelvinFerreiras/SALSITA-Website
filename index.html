About S.A.L.S.I.T.A.

S.A.L.S.I.TA is a junior executive board of the Spanish and Latino Students’ Association of the University of Rochester dedicated to teaching first year students the ins and outs of being on an executive board. Every year S.A.L.S.I.T.A. puts on events in the Fall and the Spring semester with the purpose of showcasing what our members are strongly passionate about. These activities are held for the benefit of local charities, with the desire to bring cultural, social, and educational awareness about the Spanish and Latino heritage to the campus community.
